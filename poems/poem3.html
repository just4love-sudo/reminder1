If You Ever Forgot

If you ever forgot the sound of my voice when I wasn’t trying to be strong, I hope the wind carries something familiar. Something soft. Something like me. If you forgot how I looked at you like you were more than just a person, I hope your reflection one day reminds you that someone once saw the whole universe in your eyes. If you forgot the way we laughed at nothing, the late nights, the stupid jokes, I hope a passing song pulls it all back. Even just for a moment. If you forgot how I held you without even touching you, in words, in silence, in the space between texts. I hope something aches in you the way I did. If you forgot me, not out of anger, but because life just moves on.. know that I remembered enough for both of us. And if one day the memory comes back like a whisper, like a dream, I hope it feels warm. Because it was real. It was always real.
